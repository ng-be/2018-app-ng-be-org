NG-BE 2018

NG-BE is the Belgian Angular conference and will be held at the beautiful Holiday Inn Ghent, Expo venue in Ghent on December 6 & 7, 2018.

Workshop day

December 6 is a multi-track workshop day, where attendees can learn about the latest Angular developments.

Conference day

December 7 is a single-track conference day. Attendees will be able to listen to talks from world-renowned Angular experts while enjoying delicious foods and drinks.
